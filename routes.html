Making Bus Travel Better

40+ Routes Across Lahore City

The Punjab University Transport System provides safe, affordable, and efficient bus services for students and employees, covering Lahore and its suburbs with a fleet of 55 buses. It includes regular shuttle services between Allama Iqbal and Quaid-e-Azam Campuses and supports outdoor activities like excursions and study tours at subsidized rates. The university is also expanding its fleet to enhance accessibility and comfort.
